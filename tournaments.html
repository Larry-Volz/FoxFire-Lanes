TOURNAMENTS AND WEEKLY NON-LEAGUE BOWLING EVENTS AT FOXFIRE



Starting April 5, Mondays WEEKLY at 6:30pm- THE TOUGH SHOT POTS!!!


NOT A LEAGUE! Come for any or all weeks. Rotating Tournament conditions, Weekly payoff in CASH!!! Side Pots!!! BONUS: PARTICIPANTS CAN STAY FOR $1 A GAME PRACTICE OR POTS!!!
  • Each Monday at 6:30pm starting April 5th, we will host the Tough Shot Pots.
  • Entry fee as little as $20, with several optional pots available.
  • Lane condition will be different every week, each presenting its own challenges.
  • This is open to bowlers age 13 and up, with cash prizesfor adults, converted to scholarship for youth.
  • This is a great opportunity for those wanting to better their game on tougher conditions.
  • As a bonus, those that participate can stay around after to practice or pot bowl on the tournament condition for just $1 a game.

Starting April 9, 2nd & 4th Fridays at 6:30pm



SUPER POT 2.0 NON-LEAGUE Event!
  • Come for any or all weeks.
  • We are bringing back one of our most popular fun tournaments with an added Baker Format Game.
  • Doubles Competition featuring Regular, Bakers, No-Tap, Scotch Doubles and Low Ball.
  • Note:
    • Team average may not exceed 410. Once one of our most popular Fun Tournaments, Mix or Match Doubles, bowled on our regular house conditons, now with Baker system. Each game is paid as a side pot: Regular, Bakers, No-Tap, Scotch Doubles & Low Ball.
    • Set paid 1 per 8. Entry Just $60 per team. For series award the first four games are added together, and Low Ball is subtracted for the total.
    • FYI: In Bakers the two bowlers take turns bowling frames of one game score. Scotch Doubles teammates shoot each others spares.
    • For No-Tap, 9 or 10 both count as a strike; and in low ball, the lowest score wins; but keep in mind, a miss counts as a strike or spare.

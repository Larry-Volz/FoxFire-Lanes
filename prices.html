  • SHOE RENTAL IS $4 PER PAIR AT ALL TIMES, $2.50 For Foxfire League Bowlers. Free for Foxfire Youth League Bowlers.
  • Extra Frames $.45
  • It is our intention to be open our posted hours, however; due to economic reasons we reserve the right to close as early as one hour before post if no bowlers are present. We recommend that you call to check on business if you plan on arriving after that time. We will also stay open later than our posted hours if business volume dictates.
  • NO OUTSIDE FREE GAME PASSES OR DISCOUNT CARDS MAY BE USED WHILE OTHER DISCOUNTS ARE OFFERED. THIS INCLUDES SUNDAY, WEDNESDAY, AND FRIDAY DISCOUNT DAYS. OUTSIDE IS DEFINED AS ANY DISCOUNT THAT IS NOT DISTRIBUTED DIRECTLY BY FOXFIRE EMPLOYEES. ALSO PLEASE NOTE THAT NO DISCOUNT PASSES OR CARDS MAY BE USED DURING THE MAX LIGHT AND SOUND SHOW SATURDAY NIGHTS (unless otherwise specified).
  • MOST OF THE TIME THERE IS NO NEED FOR A WAITING LIST, BUT IT IS ALWAYS A GOOD IDEA TO CALL AHEAD AND CHECK LANE AVAILABILITY. THERE IS NO WAITING LIST DURING THE MAX FOXFIRE'S WAITING LIST POLICY
  • Names may only be placed on our waiting list IN PERSON, ON THE DAY OF BOWLING. No names will be taken over the phone.
  • Lanes will be given in the order in which they are placed on the list. If a name is called, and no response is received, we will go to the next name.
  • If the absent party returns, and notifies the counter personnel, they will be called according to their original position on the list.
  • If a group has more people than will fit on their assigned lane, or lanes they will be called for available lanes until enough lanes are obtained.
  • Up to 10 people can be accommodated on one lane, but 6 is recommended.
  • It is also recommended that groups of over 10 accept two separate lanes.
  • If a lane becomes available beside either of your two lanes, you may ask for a transfer to reunite your group. If you choose not to split up, you may wait for lanes to be turned in together (the longest wait), or take one lane and add the adjoining lane "if" it comes available.
  • We would like to be able to tell you how long before you to get a lane, but we do not know. Unlike in a restaurant where people generally stay until they eat their fill, people can bowl until they drop. If we give you an estimate, understand it is based on averages, but times can fluctuate dramatically.
  • You should also understand that any posted closing time is the earliest we will close. We will stay open as long as we have enough business to permit.
  • EVERYONE WILL GET TO BOWL. On Friday & Saturday nights it is common for the center to stay open well past the scheduled closing time.
  • If you are someone that just doesn't like to wait, keep in mind that weekend evenings are our busiest times. You may avoid crowds and wait times by coming on other days, or earlier in the day. If you want a guaranteed lane, league bowling gives you just that. We have leagues for all ages, and skill levels. Get the details at our front desk.

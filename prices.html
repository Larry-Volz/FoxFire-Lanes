Simple list

  • Foxfire Bantam and Youth Leagues bowl Saturdays at 9:30am
  • Make friends and bring your own!
  • Handicapping to even out skill levels.
  • Certified and background checked coaches.
  • Optional weekly free practices on Tuesdays.
  • Free anytime shoe rental, and discounted games.
  • Discounts on bowling equipment.
  • Bantams and Bumper Bantams (age 5-8 singles) win trophies.
  • Youth (age 9-20 in 4 person teams) compete for trophies, and win scholarship money too!
  • Registration fee $20. Includes USBC Membership, Awards, Foxfire Shirt, and online standings.
  • Youth: $12 a week, bowl 3 games. Teams of 4
  • Bantams: $9 a week, bowl 2 games as single players.
  • Discounts available for full season payment.
  • Call us at 704-932-0146 to register; or click here to register online FYI: The season is 15 weeks, and is always on Saturdays. Prebowling is allowed for weeks a bowler can't attend.
  • Fees must be paid for weeks a bowler is absent too.
  • Bowlers qualify for scholarship tournament entry.
  • In recent years, 3 of our bowlers won $1800, $450, and $400 respectively. The $1800 scholarship was awarded to a first year bowler.
